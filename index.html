About

Hello, my name is Nhung! I am a computer science PhD student in Dr. Mika Rubinov's lab at Vanderbilt University. My research interests are interdisciplinary, lying at the intersection of neuroscience, genomics, and data science. My current project focuses on the genetic mechanisms that underpin functional connectivity in the human brain.

I earned a B.A. in computer science from Swarthmore College in 2019. As a first-generation college graduate, I am excited to continue connecting with and supporting other first-gen, aspiring scientists. I hope to work with educators in the future on promoting science outreach in middle and high schools. In addition to research, I love playing ultimate frisbee and visiting local coffee shops.
